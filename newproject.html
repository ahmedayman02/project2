How does dropshipping work?

No more up front inventory costs or shipping logistics. With dropshipping,
products are sent directly from your wholesaler to your customers.

Step 1

Your customer places an order from your online store

Step 2

Your store automatically sends the order to your
dropshipping supplier

Step 3

Your dropshipping supplier prepares your customer’s
order

Step 4

Your dropshipping supplier ships the order directly to
your customer
